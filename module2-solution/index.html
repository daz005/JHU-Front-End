Chicken

Description: Tender free-range chicken, seasoned with fresh herbs and grilled for a savory, slightly smoky flavor.

Ingredients: Chicken breast, olive oil, garlic, rosemary, paprika, lemon zest, salt, pepper.

Served with your choice of seasonal vegetables and sauce. Add any sides you like to round out the meal.

Beef

Description: Succulent grass-fed beef, marinated in soy and ginger, then seared to lock in all the flavor. Choose rare, medium, or well-done.

Ingredients: Beef sirloin, soy sauce, fresh ginger, sesame oil, black pepper, spring onions.

Enjoy this dish with roasted potatoes or steamed rice. We recommend a light salad on the side for extra freshness.

Sushi

Description: Classic Japanese sushi rolls made with high-quality fish, vinegared rice, and crisp nori.

Ingredients: Salmon, tuna, or eel (depending on the roll), sushi rice, nori, wasabi, soy sauce, pickled ginger.

Options include California, Spicy Tuna, and Dragon Rolls, among others. Perfect for those who appreciate fresh flavors.
