Chicken

Description: Try our tender, free-range chicken, marinated with herbs and spices, then grilled to perfection. Enjoy a savory flavor with a slight smoky twist.

Ingredients: Chicken breast, olive oil, garlic, rosemary, paprika, a dash of lemon zest, salt, pepper.

This dish is served with a side of seasonal vegetables and your choice of dipping sauce.

Beef

Description: Our premium grass-fed beef, marinated in a rich soy and ginger glaze, then quickly seared to lock in the flavor. Served rare, medium, or well-done, according to your preference.

Ingredients: Beef sirloin, soy sauce, fresh ginger, sesame oil, black pepper, spring onions.

Paired with a side of roasted potatoes or steamed rice.

Sushi

Description: Traditional Japanese sushi crafted with premium sushi-grade fish, vinegared rice, and nori, artfully rolled and presented.

Ingredients: Salmon, tuna, or eel (depending on the roll), sushi rice, nori (seaweed), wasabi, soy sauce, pickled ginger.

We offer a variety of rolls like California Roll, Spicy Tuna, Dragon Roll, and more.
